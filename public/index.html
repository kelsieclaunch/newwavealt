OUR STORY

Ever since I was a little girl, I’ve loved two things: music and helping people. Throughout my years in the music industry, I’ve noticed that the alternative scene not only lacked women, but lacked resources. From the fan-girl-to-industry pipeline: women run this industry. So let’s bring to light the “New Wave” of women in music!

Here you’ll learn about photographers, managers, agents, artists, talent buyers, tour managers, merch sellers, entrepreneurs, and so much more. This will be a space for women in learn, connect, and grow. Call it: New Wave Alternative.

- Founder, Britt Hadaway
